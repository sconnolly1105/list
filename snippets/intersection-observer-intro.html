Intersection Observer Intro

The page is broken into different sections, each section been bounded by a thick dashed border.
As you scroll down The screen and 50% of the section is in the viewport then the colour of the section will change
As you scroll further down and less than 50% is in the viewport then the section colours will change back.
Scrolling back up until 50% is again in the viewport causes the colours to be applied once again.
